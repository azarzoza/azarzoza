Hello! I am Amanda, I was born and raised in Anchorage, Alaska. I am currently a Visual Communication Design Major at Eastern Washington University. I will complete my degree in the Fall of 2015 and will also receive a minor in Communication Studies. I have always wanted to go into book or magazine publication and I think that having a background in design will help me pursue my career goals. I chose to minor in Communications because it is an area that is not my strongest skill, but I feel it will be extremely important for any path I end up going down. I want to focus on magazine and book layout design, printing and website design. I just transferred to Eastern last year, which is when I declared this major. I am still finding my way through all of the Adobe programs and getting a grasp on design.
